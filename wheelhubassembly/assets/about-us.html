Our Story

Building the Foundation of Automotive Excellence

Founded in 2001 in Chongqing, China, Axiom Drive began as a precision machining facility with a singular vision: to manufacture wheel hub assemblies that meet the exacting standards of global automotive manufacturers.

Over two decades, we have grown from a regional supplier to a global Tier-1 manufacturer with production facilities across Asia, Europe, and North America. Our journey is defined by relentless investment in technology, people, and quality systems.

Today, every third vehicle produced globally with a Chinese powertrain contains an Axiom Drive component. We remain committed to our founding principle: zero defects, zero compromises.

Milestones

Our Journey

2001

Foundation

Axiom Drive established in Chongqing, China with 50 employees and a 5,000m² facility focused on precision machining for local automotive suppliers.

2006

ISO Certification

Achieved ISO/TS 16949 certification and secured first international OEM contract with a Japanese automotive manufacturer.

2012

EV Division

Launched dedicated EV hub assembly division in response to growing electric vehicle market. First-generation EV hubs enter production.

2016

Global Expansion

Opened European headquarters in Stuttgart and North American technical center in Detroit to support regional OEM partnerships.

2020

Industry Leadership

Surpassed 100 million cumulative units produced. Recognised as top-tier supplier by multiple global automotive manufacturers.

2025

Next Generation

Fourth manufacturing base under construction in Southeast Asia. Generation IV hub platform enters final development with autonomous vehicle focus.
