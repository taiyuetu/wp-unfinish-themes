AX-3560 EV Hub Unit Enters Series Production

March 15, 2025
5 min read
Marcus Weber, VP Engineering
IMAGE: 800px × 450px

Axiom Drive is pleased to announce that the AX-3560 EV Hub Unit has entered series production at our Chongqing manufacturing facility. This third-generation hub assembly represents our most significant advancement in electric vehicle drivetrain components, delivering measurable improvements in weight, efficiency, and durability.

Background & Development

Development of the AX-3560 began in 2022 as a joint engineering initiative with a leading global BEV manufacturer. The project required a complete reimagining of hub assembly design, moving beyond traditional internal combustion vehicle architectures to address the unique requirements of battery electric platforms.

"The AX-3560 is not simply an adaptation of our existing Gen III platform—it's a purpose-built solution for the demands of high-voltage electric propulsion."

Key development priorities included minimised rotational mass to improve energy efficiency, integrated EMC shielding for high-voltage environments, and optimised seal geometry to handle the unique thermal profiles of EV applications.

Key Specifications

  • Bolt Pattern: 5×120 mm
  • Material: SAE 8620 case-hardened steel
  • Dynamic Load Rating: 56 kN
  • Weight Reduction: 18% vs. Gen II equivalent
  • Operating Temperature Range: -40°C to +150°C
  • Seal Protection: IP67 equivalent

Weight Reduction Strategy

Achieving the 18% weight reduction required a multi-faceted approach. Our engineering team employed finite element analysis to identify and eliminate non-critical material without compromising structural integrity. The redesigned bearing preload geometry not only reduces mass but also decreases friction losses by 12%.

Materials Innovation

The transition to advanced high-strength steel alloys allowed for thinner cross-sections in non-load-bearing areas while maintaining fatigue resistance. Combined with an optimised forging process developed specifically for EV applications, the result is a hub assembly that contributes meaningfully to overall vehicle range efficiency.

EMC Compliance

Electric vehicles present unique electromagnetic compatibility challenges. The AX-3560 incorporates an integrated grounding path designed into the hub forging itself, eliminating the need for additional EMC components and simplifying assembly for OEM partners.

Testing at our Detroit technical center confirmed full CISPR 25 Class 5 compliance across all operating frequencies, with margins exceeding 6 dB in critical bands.

Production & Quality

Series production commenced in Q1 2025 at our IATF 16949-certified Chongqing facility. A dedicated EV hub production line with automated assembly and 100% CMM inspection ensures consistent quality at volume.

  • Annual capacity: 2.4 million units
  • Dimensional tolerance: ±0.005 mm
  • 100% eddy current testing for surface defects
  • NVH validation on every production batch

Availability

The AX-3560 EV Hub Unit is available for OEM qualification now. Our engineering team is prepared to support integration activities and application-specific validation testing. Contact your regional Axiom Drive representative or submit an inquiry through our technical support portal.
