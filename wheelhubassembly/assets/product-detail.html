The AX-3560 EV Hub Unit represents our third-generation wheel hub assembly specifically engineered for battery electric vehicle platforms. Developed in collaboration with leading EV manufacturers, this unit addresses the unique requirements of electric propulsion including regenerative braking integration, reduced rotational mass, and extended service life.

Design Philosophy

The hub features an optimised bearing preload geometry that reduces friction losses by 18% compared to previous generations, directly contributing to improved vehicle range. The integrated ABS tone ring is machined from the hub forging, ensuring precise signal generation for the vehicle's electronic stability systems.

Materials & Manufacturing

Manufactured from premium SAE 8620 chromium-molybdenum steel with a proprietary heat treatment process achieving 58-62 HRC core hardness. The forging is produced on 4000-ton presses ensuring optimal grain flow and mechanical properties.

  • ISO 9001:2015 and IATF 16949:2016 certified
  • 100% dimensional inspection with CMM verification
  • Eddy current testing for surface defects
  • Noise, vibration, and harshness (NVH) validation

Mechanical Specifications

  • Bolt Circle Diameter: 120 mm
  • Number of Studs: 5
  • Stud Hole Diameter: 15.2 mm
  • Hub Pilot Diameter: 65.1 mm
  • Flange Thickness: 22 mm
  • Overall Length: 78 mm
  • Dynamic Load Rating: 56 kN
  • Static Load Rating: 89 kN

Material Properties

  • Material: SAE 8620 Case-Hardened Steel
  • Case Depth: 1.2-1.8 mm
  • Surface Hardness: 58-62 HRC
  • Core Hardness: 30-40 HRC
  • Surface Roughness: Ra 0.8 μm

Seal Specifications

  • Seal Type: Triple-Lip Contact Seal
  • Seal Material: FKM (Fluoroelastomer)
  • Operating Temperature: -40°C to +150°C
  • Dust Protection: IP67 Equivalent
  • Grease Fill: 25g ± 2g (NLGI Grade 2)
